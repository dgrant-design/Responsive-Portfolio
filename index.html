About Me

Hello my name is Darwin Grant, I am currently employed at the Blue Island Oyster Bar. Before the Oyster Bar I received my Bachelors Degree from the University of Colorado Denver in International Studies and a minor in Ethnic Studies. I chose this degree because I wanted to learn more about the world we live in and the cultures that make us all unique. I developed a passion for not recognizing the difference between people, but more so what people had in common besides their differences.
